# Moses - Product Lead / Founder

## Owns

- Product vision and scope control
- Final sprint decisions
- Customer demo narrative
- MVP tradeoff calls
- Validation questions for manufacturing leaders

## Tasks

- Define the Day 3 demo story around the morning production meeting.
- Keep the team focused on the core workflow: data, risks, brief, owner questions.
- Decide which features are in or out when scope pressure appears.
- Review the generated brief and confirm it answers: "What needs my attention today?"
- Prepare the customer-test script and feedback questions.
- Approve the final sample scenario before demo.

## Done When

- The sprint has a clear demo narrative.
- The repo does not drift into platform creep.
- The MVP is easy to explain to a manufacturing leader in under two minutes.
